TicTacToe2D

The classic pass-time game of all time.Also called circles and cross. The given game has two modes a single played (where you compete against an unbeatable AI) and two player where two people can compete with each other.

Basic Gameplay

  • There are 3*3 matrix
  • p1 is circle ad p2 is cross
  • players takes turn to choose a box to fill with their respective symbol
  • if a player gets a horizontal, vertical or diagonal match of their symbols, they win
  • Else declared as draw match
