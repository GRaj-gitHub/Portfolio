Splash-It-On

Splash-It-On is a demo version of the famous nintendo game "Splatoon". the game is multiplayer where two teams are competing with each other to fill the maximum amount of area with their respective paints in the given time while having limited no of lives. There are power-ups to help them. For now the game is offline and so is played with different controls in the same system. more team options, challenges and online option for multiplayer is the next targeted update.
